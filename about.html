About This Site

I work at the intersection of AI, neurodata, and sleep research, focusing on EEG signal analysis and physiological data modeling.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering and a Master’s degree in Business Analytics, with experience in machine learning, biosignal processing, and data visualization.

My current interests include sleep cycle detection, EEG-based pattern discovery, and developing open-source tools that make brain and health data more accessible to researchers and students.
